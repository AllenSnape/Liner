一、如果你能翻墙出去
1. 正常访问 https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/liner/giejbolgkjblhpnilfailflgdbhikjme
2. 点击添加至chrome
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC0.jpg[/img]
3. 如下
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC1.jpg[/img]
4. 完成安装之后, 再访问蜂鸟网, 将图片拖拽到"读取区"即可
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC2.jpg[/img]


二、如果你翻不出去这堵墙
1. 正常访问 GitHub: https://github.com/AllenSnape/Liner
2. 点击绿色按钮 Clone or download
3. 然后再点击 Download ZIP, 然后解压出来, 解压的地址自己能找得到就好
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%B0%8F%E7%99%BD%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC0.jpg[/img]
4. 点击谷歌浏览器右上角的三个点, 然后"更多工具"→"扩展程序"
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%B0%8F%E7%99%BD%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC1.jpg[/img]
5. 点击"开发者模式"
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%B0%8F%E7%99%BD%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC2.jpg[/img]
6. 点击"加载已解压的扩展程序…"
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%B0%8F%E7%99%BD%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC3.jpg[/img]
7. 然后出现以下页面
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%B0%8F%E7%99%BD%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC4.jpg[/img]
8. 访问蜂鸟网, 将图片拖拽到"读取区"即可
[img]https://raw.githubusercontent.com/AllenSnape/Liner/master/publishing/chrome%E5%BC%95%E5%AF%BC2.jpg[/img]
